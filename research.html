Research

Exploring biological systems through interdisciplinary research integrating molecular biology, ecology, genomics, computational biology and biotechnology.

Research Overview

Our laboratory investigates biological questions from genes to ecosystems using modern laboratory, field and computational techniques.

We combine molecular experiments, high-throughput sequencing, artificial intelligence, ecological modeling and bioinformatics to better understand living systems.

Our interdisciplinary collaborations allow us to translate biological discoveries into solutions for healthcare, agriculture and environmental sustainability.
