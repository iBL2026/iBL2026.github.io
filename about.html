About Our Laboratory

Integrating biology, technology and innovation to solve complex biological challenges.

Who We Are

The Integrative Biology Laboratory is a multidisciplinary research group dedicated to understanding life across multiple biological scales—from molecules and cells to organisms and ecosystems.

Our laboratory combines experimental biology, computational approaches, artificial intelligence, genomics and ecological research to answer important scientific questions.

Mission & Vision

Mission

Conduct world-class biological research while training future scientists through innovation, collaboration and education.

Vision

Become a globally recognized research laboratory advancing biological discoveries that improve human and environmental health.

Values

Integrity, collaboration, diversity, scientific excellence and open research.
