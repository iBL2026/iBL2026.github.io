About Our Laboratory

The Integrative Biology Laboratory brings together researchers from molecular biology, ecology, genetics, computational biology and environmental science to understand biological systems from genes to ecosystems.

Our mission is to conduct innovative research, educate future scientists and develop sustainable solutions for global biological challenges.
